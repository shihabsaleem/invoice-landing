Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

1. Overview

Invoice by jadbery is a 100% free, offline-first invoice generator designed for speed and professionalism. No sign-up required, no hidden fees, and your data stays on your device.

We value your privacy. This extension operates offline-first. We do not transmit or store your sensitive financial data on our servers.

2. Data Collection

Because our application runs entirely client-side (in your browser), we technically cannot see or collect the following:

  • Your client lists or details.
  • Invoice amounts, dates, or items.
  • Your personal or business contact information.
  • Payment details or banking information.

3. Data Storage

All your client data, company details, and invoice history are stored securely in your browser's localStorage or indexedDB.

If you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data, this local information may be deleted. We recommend downloading PDFs of your invoices for permanent records.

4. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services to ensure the extension functions correctly:

  • Google Fonts: Used to load typography.
  • Chrome Web Store: Handles the distribution and updates of the extension.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
